### 👋 Hey, I'm Jobal

Civil Engineer specializing in **miscellaneous steel detailing** — stairs, handrails, ladders, platforms — modeled in **Tekla Structures**.

When something in my workflow is slow or repetitive, I build a tool to kill it. AI writes most of the code. I just know exactly what problem needs solving.

**Not a programmer.** Just someone who refuses to do the same thing twice.
